Kia ora

my name is Sam i was born and raised in Te-Whanganui-A-Tara but have relocated up to Tamaki Makaurau . Growing up I adored films, and video games. I had a fasciation with how they worked and always thought they people behind them where magicians. This led to a love of being creative in way shape or form. This lead me to study Creative Media Production at Massey, Learning all about Film and Game design.

I have spent 10 years working in Hospitality. I loved everything about it and still adore cooking and making food. However I wanted a change from the heat of the kitchen lifestyle. Talking with customers and old work friends led me to get interested in a world outside the kitchen This lead to a deep dive in the world of coding and web development which became something I wanted to get stuck into. Along came Dev Academy which I see as the perfect challenge to help kickstart myself into a new career.

I know the road will be hard but I'm so excited to get stuck in, meet all new people and bring my creative mind into a whole new world!

Sam.
